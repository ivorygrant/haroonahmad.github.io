Projects

My Twitter Sentiment Analyzer

Are you curious how positive or negative a user's tweets are? This bot collects the last 500 tweets of a target user and plots a score showing how positive or negative their tweets are. This project uses Vader Sentiment Analysis to analyze text and Matplotlib to graph their scores.

Is Music Becoming More Negative?

Have you ever felt that music has become more negative compared to days past? In this project, we use Vader Sentiment Analysis on Billboard Top 100 songs for the last 50 years, and analyze their scores. The results may shock you.

View Project

The Opioid Crisis in America: A Visual Representation

The Opioid Crisis has gripped the US, and has led lawmakers, health advocates, and even the President to declare it a national emergency. This project gives users a visual representation of this crisis, plotting data from 1999 to 2016.

How Serious is Your Hepatitis?

In this project, we use data on Hepatitis patients to predict if they are at risk of death from Hepatitis. Based on our Machine Learning model, we developed an app that allows doctors and health professionals to input categorical and numerical data about their patients, to predict their risk from this disease.
